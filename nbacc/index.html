nba comeback calculator#

What lead is safe and what comeback is possible?#

To examine this question, I downloaded all games from when play by play was available (from the 1996-1997 season to now) and look at the percent times games were won versus varying conditions.

  • analysis: Looks at some trends in the data and tries to answer the question “are big comebacks just much more common nowadays?”

  • methodology: Covers how this data was put together.

  • plots: Provides a scrollalbe webpage of many different comeback plots (including ones for our Minnesota Timberwolves!).

  • interactive: Provides a configurable plotting tool so you can slice and dice the data as you like (including for you favorite team).
